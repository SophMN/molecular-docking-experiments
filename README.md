#Molecular docking experiments

Molecular docking modeling technique that studies how two or more molecular structures fit together. It predicts how a receptor molecule \
(e.g. protein) interacts with and binds to a ligand (e.g. small molecule or a drug). Docking aims to identify the correct poses of ligands \
in the binding pocket of a receptor and predict the binding affinity between the receptor and the ligand.
